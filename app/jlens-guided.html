From the anchor, the model rewrites the story twice — the only difference between the two rewrites is the swap. Words already written before the anchor stay as they are (including the swapped word itself, if it's there): the swap changes what the model is thinking from the anchor on, not the text it already wrote.

J-lens rows show what the middle of the network is disposed to say; the logit lens shows what it would blurt out if it stopped there. DIFF marks words only the J-lens sees (green). Pink chips are name fragments — “L… (Lily?)” means the model is thinking of a word that starts like Lily. Ranks are within the story vocabulary (~5k words).
